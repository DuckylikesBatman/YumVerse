CORPORATE OVERVIEW

YumVerse is a modern pizza kitchen based in Beirut, dedicated to crisp, airy, long-fermented dough and bold, seasonal flavors. We serve dine-in, takeaway, delivery, and catering, with a guest experience designed for speed, warmth, and consistency.

Mission

Make great pizza easy to love—every day—by combining craft baking, quality ingredients, and thoughtful hospitality.

What We Do
